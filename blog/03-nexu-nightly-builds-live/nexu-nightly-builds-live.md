# nexu v0.1.3 Nightly: Automated Test Builds Are Live

> nexu now ships automated nightly test builds — notarized, stapled, and ready for early testing.

Automated desktop build.

**Version:** 0.1.3
**Environment:** test
**Commit:** c5fb86e9ff38ae7b18ac00dd19d5350834602651
**Built:** 20260319

The packaged Nexu.app bundle is notarized and stapled.

This build is intended for testing and may be unstable.

## Three Release Channels

We maintain three release channels: **Nightly** (latest code, may be unstable) for developers who want to follow along closely. **Beta** (feature-complete, needs testing) for power users who want early access. **Stable** (production-ready) for daily business use.

## How to Use It

Download the nightly .dmg from our [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/nexu-io/nexu/releases), install it alongside your stable version, and report any issues you find.
